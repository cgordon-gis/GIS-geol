    Welcome to GIS for Geologists

    These pages are the exercises for the Introduction to GIS course for geology students at the University of Leeds.

    The principles behind the use of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) will be provided as materials in Minerva. These materials will explain why you are doing the exercises. These webpages will explain how to carry out the tasks.

    Use the links to navigate through the pages.

    These web pages will be kept updated as the course progresses. If you would prefer a version for printing please download the Word or pdf version. Note that these may not be quite as up to date.

    If you need the instructions in a different format please contact me. Details in Minerva
